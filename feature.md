# MosqueConnect Features & Services

MosqueConnect is a comprehensive platform designed to facilitate communication, management, and governance for local mosques and central Shura Councils. Below is a detailed breakdown of all the services and modules available in the application.

## 1. Mosque Directory & Discovery
- **Mosque Profiles:** Comprehensive details including address, contact info, facilities, and established year.
- **Location-Based Discovery:** Find nearby mosques using geographic coordinates.
- **Verified Status:** Badges for mosques that have been verified by the central Shura Council.
- **Mosque Registration Portal:** A pipeline for new mosques to join the network, including document upload and review processes.

## 2. Prayer Times Service
- **Daily Prayer Times:** Schedules for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha.
- **Iqama Tracking:** Specific times for congressional prayer (Iqama).
- **Jummah Schedules:** Dedicated scheduling for Friday prayers.
- **Auto-Calculation:** Integration with algorithmic APIs to automatically determine prayer times based on location.

## 3. Event & Program Management
- **Event Catalog:** Categories for Jummah, lectures, classes, Quran study, youth programs, fundraisers, and more.
- **Recurring Events:** Support for daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly programs.
- **Attendance Tracking:** Capacity limits and current attendee counting.
- **Promotional Media:** Support for event posters and speaker details.

## 4. Community Feed & Announcements
- **Notice Board:** Pinned announcements and urgent alerts.
- **Categorized Posts:** Announcements sorted by general, prayer, event, community, or educational categories.
- **Expiry Dates:** Automated removal of outdated announcements.

## 5. Personnel & Leadership Portal
- **Imam Profiles:** Detailed biographies, education history, specializations, teaching styles, and available services (e.g., counseling, Nikah).
- **Imam Weekly Schedules:** Publicly visible office hours and activity schedules.
- **Management Committees:** Profiles for mosque board members, trustees, and presidents with their specific responsibilities and term limits.

## 6. Shura Council Governance (Higher Administration)
- **Shura Member Directory:** Profiles of central governance officials and the regions they oversee.
- **Mosque Visits & Assessments:** A system to schedule, conduct, and log routine inspections and meetings at local mosques.
- **Mosque Rating System:** Standardized ratings across cleanliness, prayer services, education, youth programs, and financial transparency.
- **Meeting Management:** Complete workflow for scheduling Shura Council meetings (virtual or physical) including agenda items, attendance, and action tracking.
- **Imam Appointment System:** Pipeline for interviewing, reviewing, and appointing new Imams to mosques.

## 7. Library Management
- **Book Cataloging:** Track books by category (Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, etc.), ISBN, author, and physical shelf location.
- **Inventory Status:** Real-time checking of total vs. available copies.
- **Borrowing System:** Track who borrowed which book, check-out dates, due dates, and overdue status.
- **Condition Monitoring:** Track the physical condition of items (new, excellent, fair, poor).
- **Equipment Library:** Manage non-book items like audio equipment, prayer mats, and furniture.

## 8. Finance & Donations
- **Fundraising Goals:** Create campaigns outlining target amounts and tracking current progress.
- **Donation Records:** Maintain logs of incoming Zakat, Sadaqah, and building funds (both anonymous and public).
- **Expense Tracking:** Manage operational costs, utilities, maintenance, and salary records.
- **Receipts & Transparency:** Automated logging for financial accountability.
